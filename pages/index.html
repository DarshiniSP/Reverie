The Problem

Productivity apps solve the wrong problem.

"Important goals coexist with trivial errands in the same list, with no indication that one has been untouched for six weeks."

They are excellent at capture — getting things out of your head and into a list. But they stop there. Once a task is entered, the app becomes passive. It waits. It reminds. It does not reason.

👁️

The Visibility Problem

The gap between intention and action is not a motivation problem — it is a visibility problem. Nothing flags that your relationships domain has had zero activity in a month.

🔄

Productive Drift

You can feel productive completing small easy tasks while the one hard thing that actually matters keeps getting rescheduled — indefinitely.

🔥

Streak Trackers Miss the Point

They track streaks, not meaning. They reward consistency without asking whether what you are consistent about aligns with who you are trying to become.

🗺️

What Was Missing

A layer of intelligence over the whole picture — something that understood the structure of a life, not just a task list.

The Solution

Three interconnected layers

Reverie is not a task manager. It is a life management system built around three layers that work together to close the gap between intention and action.

1
📐

Structure

A framework for organising life across 8 domains with Plans, Journeys, and Routines that reflect how real goals actually work — not flat lists, but hierarchies with milestones, deadlines, and recurring practices.

2
🧠

Intelligence

Lumina runs proactively in the background, analysing your task and goal data every 6 hours, detecting patterns — goal drift, domain silence, burnout risk — and surfacing personalised nudges grounded in your actual behaviour.

3
✨

Reflection

A Growth Mindset Engine that tracks not just completions but recovery — giving credit for coming back after a miss, measuring resilience, and framing your data in terms of growth rather than failure.

🏠 Today View

Your day, intelligently surfaced

The Today view is your operational centre — not a passive list, but an intelligent dashboard that understands your cognitive load, surfaces what matters most, and keeps your routines front and centre.

Cognitive Load Indicator — Reverie tracks how many tasks are in scope, across how many domains, and warns you when you're overloaded before the day gets away from you.

Overdue visibility — Overdue tasks surface with context: how late, which domain, what priority — so nothing gets quietly forgotten.

Routines with streak tracking — Daily, weekly, and custom-interval routines appear with live streak counts, break history, and recovery context.

Resilience Index snapshot — Your live wellbeing score with trend direction, always visible from the home screen.

⊞ Domains & Goals

Structure that reflects how life actually works

Every task in Reverie belongs to a life domain. Domains are organised through Plans (high-level buckets), Journeys (time-bound goals with milestones), and Routines — turning a flat checklist into a map of your life.

8 fixed life domains — Health, Career, Relationships, Learning, Creativity, Finance, Home, Personal. Fixed domains enable Lumina to reason meaningfully about balance.

Journeys with milestones — Goals are time-bound with visual milestone timelines, progress bars, and due dates — not just names on a list.

Domain progress — See tasks done this week, active count, and overall domain health at a glance — across all 5 custom domains shown in your Domains view.

Forcing function — Assigning a domain forces the question: what is this task actually in service of? That turns a checklist into a map of your intentions.

Goal Drift Detection

Flags when journeys have had no supporting task activity recently, signalling goals being crowded out by daily urgency.

Privacy & Security

Privacy should be structural, not advisory

Reverie handles genuinely personal data — daily routines, life goals, reflections. Privacy is an engineering constraint from the start, not a feature bolted on at the end.

💾

Local-First Storage

All task, goal, and routine data is stored locally using SwiftData. Optional iCloud sync via CloudKit. Nothing is sent to a server by default.

🔏

Automatic PII Scrubber

Names, locations, and sensitive content are automatically redacted before any data leaves the device for inference calls. Privacy is structural, not advisory.

👁️

Biometric Authentication

Face ID / Touch ID locks the app, with a one-hour idle re-lock to prevent casual shoulder-surfing. Your data is yours.

🔑

Keychain API Storage

API keys are stored in the iOS Keychain and never leave the device in plaintext. Monthly token budget caps prevent unexpected API spend.

How It Was Built

Four deliberate iterations

Intelligence without structure produces noise. Structure without intelligence produces a more organised whiteboard. Each version answered a different question.

v1

Core Task Structure

Tasks, Plans, and the 8-domain framework. Validated that structured life organisation across domains was more useful than flat lists. No intelligence layer yet.

v2

Journeys & Routines

Added goal tracking with milestones and recurring practices with streak logic. The structure-first principle emerged: intelligence without structure produces noise.

v3

Lumina — Capture & Knowledge

Added conversational AI — chat-based task capture, semantic memory search, and knowledge notes indexed for future retrieval. Solved the capture friction problem.

v4

Proactive Intelligence & Security

The most significant iteration: background intelligence engine, daily briefing, Growth Mindset analytics, PII scrubbing, biometric lock, and token budget management. Answered the core question: can software notice what you're missing before you do?
